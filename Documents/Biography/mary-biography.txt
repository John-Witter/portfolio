Mary Witlacil is a PhD candidate in the Department of Political Science
at Colorado State University. She is a 2022-2023 SOGES Leadership Fellow
who serves on the Executive Committee for the Environmental Politics and
Theory Related Group for the American Political Science Association.
She is both a critical theorist and an environmental political theorist 
whose dissertation research centers around developing a theory of radical 
ecopessimism. She has expertise in environmental justice, decolonial 
political theory, affect, the politics of climate change, as well as 
contemporary and historical political thought. So far, her work has been 
published in //New Political Science//.
